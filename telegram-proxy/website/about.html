About NexaHost

We build hosting infrastructure that developers and businesses can depend on.

Our mission

NexaHost was founded in 2018 by a team of infrastructure engineers who were frustrated with the gap between enterprise-grade hosting and what small teams could afford. We set out to change that.

Today we operate 18 data centers across three continents, serve over 12,000 customers, and maintain a 99.97% uptime track record — all while keeping our pricing transparent and our support human.

What we believe

  • Hosting should be simple, not simplified.
  • You should never have to read a 40-page bill to know what you owe.
  • Support should be engineers, not scripts.
  • Infrastructure is the foundation — we take that seriously.
